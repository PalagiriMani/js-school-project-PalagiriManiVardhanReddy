Historical Milestones

1903: The First Powered Flight

The Wright brothers, Orville and Wilbur, achieve the first successful sustained and controlled powered flight, marking the birth of the aerospace age.

1947: Breaking the Sound Barrier

Chuck Yeager, flying the Bell X-1, becomes the first human to officially fly faster than the speed of sound, opening the door to supersonic travel.

1969: The Apollo 11 Moon Landing

NASA's Apollo 11 mission lands the first humans on the Moon, a monumental achievement for humanity and the peak of the Space Race.

1981: The First Reusable Spacecraft

The launch of the Space Shuttle Columbia introduced the era of reusable spacecraft, designed to make access to low-Earth orbit more routine.

2015: The First Reusable Rocket Landing

SpaceX successfully landed the first stage of its Falcon 9 rocket, a revolutionary step towards dramatically lowering the cost of access to space.
